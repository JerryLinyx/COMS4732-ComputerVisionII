HW2: Automatic Feature Matching Across Images

Author: Yuxuan Lin (yl6061@columbia.edu), assisted by GPT-5.2
Step choices
  • Step 1 (Harris): OpenCV cornerHarris with block_size=2, ksize=3, k=0.04. Visualization corners are 3x3 local maxima above a relative threshold.
  • Step 2 (NMS): Local-maximum suppression in a square window sized by image scale; window sizes are reported per image.
  • Step 3 (Descriptor): 40x40 grayscale patch, Gaussian blur (anti-alias), downsample to 8x8, bias/gain normalize (mean 0, std 1).
  • Step 4 (Matching): SSD (L2) on descriptors with optional L2-normalization, NNDR thresholding, plus mutual nearest-neighbor filtering.
  • Extra credit: Use a looser matching config for RANSAC homography, then warp + feather blend. Simple overlap-based color gain/bias correction is applied before blending.
