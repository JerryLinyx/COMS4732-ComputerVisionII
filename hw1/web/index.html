Introduction

This page presents results from COMS4732 Computer Vision II Homework 1, aligning the Prokudin-Gorskii glass plates.
Blue is the reference channel. Reported shifts are (dy, dx) applied to the moving channel to align with Blue.
Single-scale searches ±15 px; the pyramid halves resolution until min_dim < 400, then refines with ±2 px windows.

Solution

  • Score: NCC (Normalized Cross Correlation) on gradient magnitude (Sobel) after mean-centering; interior crop of 12% to suppress borders. L2 is not used.
  • Single-scale: exhaustive search around (0,0) within ±15 px (for low-res JPG baseline).
  • Pyramid: 2×2 average downsampling; coarse search ±25 px at coarsest level, then ±2 px refinement per level.
  • Bells & Whistles: gradient-based NCC (illumination-robust), adaptive bit-depth normalization, larger interior crop, downsampled pyramid.

Runtime: ~2 minutes for the full batch on the provided machine; outputs land in web/assets.
